Official Development Financing (ODF), measured for recipient countries only,
is defined as the sum of their receipts of bilateral ODA,
concessional and non-concessional resources from multilateral sources,
and bilateral other official flows made available for reasons unrelated to trade,
in particular loans to refinance debt.
